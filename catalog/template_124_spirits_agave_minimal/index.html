The Origin

Born in the
Highlands

At 7,000 feet above sea level, the red clay soil of Los Altos de Jalisco produces agave with higher sugar content and richer flavor notes. {{COMPANY_NAME}} is the result of three generations of jimadores who understand the land's rhythm.

We wait 7 to 9 years for each piña to reach peak maturity before harvesting by hand. No diffusers, no additives—just pure, unadulterated agave spirit.

From Earth to Bottle

01

The Harvest

Our jimadores hand-select only the ripest piñas, shaving them close to the heart to remove bitterness.

02

Slow Roasting

Cooked in traditional brick ovens for 72 hours to convert starches into fermentable sugars, unlocking deep caramel notes.

03

Double Distillation

Distilled twice in copper pot stills to refine the spirit while preserving the bright, herbal character of the agave.
