How We Work

The Fabrication Process

01

Design

We digitize your concept and create vector blueprints for bending.

02

Bending

Glass tubes are heated to 1200°F and hand-shaped by master benders.

03

Pumping

Air is removed and replaced with noble gases (Neon or Argon) to create light.

04

Installation

The sign is mounted, wired, and tested for brilliance and longevity.
